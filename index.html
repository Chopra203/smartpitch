About us

What is SmartPitch Analysis ?

At SmartPitch Analysis, we believe every football team deserves access to high-quality performance insights. Our solutions are flexible, designed to fit any budget, and customizable to your team’s specific goals. Whether you’re looking for full match reports, key player analysis, or in-depth positional data, SmartPitch brings actionable insights that make a difference.

At SmartPitch Analysis, we believe every football team—regardless of size or budget—should have access to professional-grade performance analysis. Our mission is to empower clubs with customized insights that meet their unique goals, enabling smarter decisions both on and off the pitch.

How it works

Our 3 step process

STEP

1

Initial Contact and Consultation

Once you reach out to us, we’ll set up a consultation to discuss your specific needs and goals. Our team will work with you to understand your coaching style, team objectives, and the type of insights you're looking for.

Deliverable: A clear understanding of your requirements and preferred Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

Goal: To align our analysis with your coaching strategy and team’s objectives.

STEP

2

Customized KPI & Analysis Plan

After the initial consultation, we collaborate with you to design a custom analysis plan, setting key performance indicators that reflect your priorities. This step ensures that our reports are not only insightful but directly relevant to your team’s development.

Deliverable: A tailored list of KPIs and an analysis outline designed to meet your unique needs.

Goal: To maximize relevance and ensure actionable insights.

STEP

3

Data Collection, Analysis, and Reporting

With the customized plan in place, we begin collecting and analyzing data according to your selected package. Our team uses advanced tools, like Metrica and Tableau, to compile video and data insights that translate directly to the field.

Deliverable: Comprehensive reports, video highlights, and data-driven insights sent via your preferred method (PDF, WhatsApp, or online presentation).

Goal: To deliver clear, actionable feedback that enhances your team’s performance on the field. Call to Action
